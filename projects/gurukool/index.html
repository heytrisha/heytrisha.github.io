Goal

“How might we help parents support their child’s learning journey in a guided, stress-free way?”

Research

Understanding the problem space

To understand parents’ and students’ homework habits, challenges, and expectations, I used two key research methods.

User Interviews

Interviewed 10 working and stay-at-home parents to better understand challenges, behaviors, and needs when supporting their children with schoolwork.

Key questions asked

  • 01Can you walk me through a typical day when your child has homework?
  • 02What kinds of subjects or topics do you find easy or difficult to help with?
  • 03What challenges do you face when supporting your child with homework?
  • 04How much time do you spend helping with homework?
  • 05Have you ever felt stuck or unsure about how to explain a concept?
  • 06What tools do you currently use?
  • 07Would you trust AI-based homework help?
  • 08Are there any emotional challenges, like stress or guilt, during homework time?
  • 09Do you ever look for outside help (online resources, tutors, other parents)?
  • 10Have you ever wanted to connect with other parents or experts for advice?
  • 11What would an ideal support system or community for parents look like to you?

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed 4 popular homework and learning support platforms (BYJU’S, Brainly, Toppr, and Vedantu) to understand existing solutions, identify feature gaps, and uncover opportunities to design a more parent-inclusive, guided learning experience.

Key Findings

What was discovered

Parents face time constraints and low confidence when helping with homework.

They prefer quick, reliable support over complex solutions.

Learning is fragmented across multiple tools, leading to lost progress.

Existing platforms are student-centric, with minimal parent support.

Trust in AI depends on clarity, transparency, and ease of use.

From Research to Design

How insights shaped the experience

Community Support

Enable peer interaction through topic-based discussion spaces so parents never feel alone in the journey.

Personalized Experience

Deliver tailored content based on curriculum, grade, and progress so help is always relevant.

Trustworthy AI

Provide instant, reliable assistance through a simple and transparent chatbot that explains, not just answers.

Unified Platform

Consolidate learning, tracking, and support into a single seamless experience, no more juggling apps.
