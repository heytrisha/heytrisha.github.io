      <p class="text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">Existing platforms are <strong class="text-[#fafafa]">student-centric</strong>, with minimal parent support.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="border border-white/10 rounded-xl p-6 bg-white/[0.02] md:col-span-2">
