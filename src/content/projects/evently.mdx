  {/* Project Overview */}
  <div class="mb-16">
    <p class="text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-[#fafafa] mb-6 leading-tight text-center">
      Discover, Create, Belong!
    </p>
    <p class="text-2xl md:text-3xl text-gray-300 leading-relaxed font-light mb-8">
      Evently is an event discovery platform designed to make finding and hosting experiences more joyful, personalized, and efficient for two key personas: <strong class="text-[#fafafa]">Explorers</strong> and <strong class="text-[#fafafa]">Creators / Hosts</strong>.
    </p>
    <p class="text-gray-400 text-lg leading-relaxed mb-8">

    </p>

  {/* Challenges */}
  <div class="mb-16">
    <h2 class="text-sm uppercase tracking-widest text-gray-500 mb-4">Challenges</h2>
    <h3 class="text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-[#fafafa] mb-8 leading-tight">
      Where It Hurt
    </h3>

    <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
      <div class="border-l border-l-white/20 pl-6">
        <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Overwhelming discovery</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 leading-relaxed">
          Users often face endless event listings without meaningful personalization, making it hard to surface what's relevant.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="border-l border-l-white/20 pl-6">
        <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Limited filtering</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 leading-relaxed">
          Most platforms rely on basic filters like location and date, making it difficult to narrow down relevant events.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="border-l border-l-white/20 pl-6">
        <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Decision fatigue</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 leading-relaxed">
          With too many options and little guidance, users struggle to choose events confidently and abandon the search.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="border-l border-l-white/20 pl-6">
        <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Low visibility for creators</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 leading-relaxed">
          Hosts often rely on social media promotion to reach audiences, with few built-in tools to grow their community.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="border-l border-l-white/20 pl-6">
        <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Communication gap</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 leading-relaxed">
          Users cannot easily ask questions or interact with hosts before booking, reducing trust and commitment.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="border-l border-l-white/20 pl-6">
        <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Lack of emotional engagement</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 leading-relaxed">
          Existing platforms feel transactional rather than inspiring, missing the excitement that drives attendance.
        </p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  {/* Key Learnings */}
  <div class="mb-16">
    <h2 class="text-sm uppercase tracking-widest text-gray-500 mb-4">Key Learnings</h2>
    <h3 class="text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-[#fafafa] mb-8 leading-tight">
      Lessons in Discovery
    </h3>

    <div class="grid md:grid-cols-3 gap-4">
      <div class="rounded-lg p-5 bg-violet-500/[0.08]">
        <h4 class="text-lg font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Emotion matters</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 text-sm leading-relaxed">
          Experiences should feel exciting, not purely functional. Emotional engagement drives discovery and loyalty.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="rounded-lg p-5 bg-violet-500/[0.08]">
        <h4 class="text-lg font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Reduce cognitive load</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 text-sm leading-relaxed">
          Discovery products must not force users to scroll endlessly. Smart filtering and personalization are essential.
        </p>
      </div>
      <div class="rounded-lg p-5 bg-violet-500/[0.08]">
        <h4 class="text-lg font-semibold text-[#fafafa] mb-2">Balance both sides</h4>
        <p class="text-gray-400 text-sm leading-relaxed">
          Designing for two user groups requires careful balance, explorers want inspiration, while creators want control and visibility.
        </p>
      </div>
    </div>

  {/* Reflection */}
  <div class="mb-8">
    <h2 class="text-sm uppercase tracking-widest text-gray-500 mb-4">Reflection</h2>
    <p class="text-gray-400 text-lg leading-relaxed">
      Evently reinforced how thoughtful UX design can transform everyday tasks like event discovery into meaningful experiences. By designing for two distinct user groups, explorers seeking inspiration and creators seeking visibility, the project highlighted the importance of balancing emotional engagement with practical utility. The biggest takeaway was that reducing cognitive load through smarter personalization and filtering is not just a convenience, but the core of a joyful discovery experience.
    </p>
